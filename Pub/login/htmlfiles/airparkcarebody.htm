To Do List

 We all share a very large front yard. The most important question we can all ask  is "What can I do to help"?

Currently, the most authoritative source of information in this regard comes from Phil and Katie Dater.

We have recently added a basic "To Do" list (to your left) - currently this is very basic. There is no authentication, and anyone can update, modify (and trash if you're not careful). When an item is marked complete, it get's placed in the archive, and can later be pulled out of the archive. Use the "search" and "keyword" mechanism to refine your display. When you add new items, "tree" them, and add keywords to make the search more effective. Because there is no authentication, you can bookmark this page and go directly to it without having to first log in to the BMA website.
