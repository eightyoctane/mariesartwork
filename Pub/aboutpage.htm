Art has always been an integral part of my life. As a young child growing up in Germany, my “Opa” (grandfather) and I would wander the halls of art museums. Even at that young age, I was in awe of the great masters and especially moved by the Fauve artists – impressionistic rebels of their time. Maybe it is for this reason that I consider myself a rebel, a nonconformist.

Each collection that I paint is a journey. In each journey, I want to experience everything that makes up this environment – all of the sights, sounds and smells. After researching the location, I visit the site to make sketches and take pictures. Using all that I experience, I paint from the heart, from my intuitive self, inspired by color and textures and shaped in nature.


There is so much sadness in the world that I want my work to be a positive influence. If I can make people feel good through the use of color and texture, then I have been successful. Come journey with me as I discover the world!

Marie House
