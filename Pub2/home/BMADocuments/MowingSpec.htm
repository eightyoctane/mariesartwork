BMA Mowing and Bush Hogging Specification and Checklist
Notes:
  1. Expect Mowing Maintenance ~6mos/year
  2. Owners currently participate in mowing and estimated times are those they have come to expect for each category
  3. Use this checklist to identify tasks accomplished. Submit at end-of-job
  4. Two zero-turn mowers (52 and 72), and a community weedeater are available for this work, and must be maintained by the vendor.
  5. Gas must be supplied by the vendor.
  6. Gas powered equipment must be topped off by the vendor at completion of the job.
  7. Oil and lubricant will be supplied by the community
