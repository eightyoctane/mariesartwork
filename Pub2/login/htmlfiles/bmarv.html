BMA Visitors Area

 Version 1.0 09-11-2011
 
Currently BMA registered visitors have access to the Projects area of the website. Soon we will have a BMA "friends" category which will give BMA friends access to the airpark wall.

There are a number of good reasons to have BMA wall privileges. BMA resident/owners monitor the wall more frequently than email, sharing thoughts, fun ideas, and upcoming activities. Because many of us live "in the neighborhood" some of the activities are very last minute (cookout, wine tasting, R/C Helicopter flights, airplane rides).

If you want information fast, having wall privileges is the way to go (like: What are the conditions at the airstrip? Are you really having a pancake breakfast on Saturday? If I get stuck at the airstrip, is there a place for me to stay?)

Many of those with wall privileges have email notifications set for new wall entries.

If you want  friend and wall privileges, you will need a reference (resident/owner) contact who will vouch for you. Just add this contact info when you register or update your account.

Other airpark residents and owners can obtain wall privileges by contacting a BMA board member (mariesartwork@yahoo.com). This is one of our visions for the airpark small-community-website. We would like to see airparks all around the country share these capabilities, and share our collective advantage.

Stay tuned for this upcoming "friends" feature!
