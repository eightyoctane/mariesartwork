How to Add Photos (and Albums) to the Gallery

We have three different methods of uploading photos to the website, which is the result of some evolution (and it's still evolving). The latest and most granular method is Picasa.

Latest method!

We have several photo streams now and we're expanding as I have time to explore further. Currently all is served by Adobe (bad news for Apple Mobile Users), and should be viewable on all PCs, non-mobile Apple products, and non Apple Mobile Devices as well (so there ya go, Apple dislikes Adobe and doesn't play well with their competitive partners, even though, like Microsoft, Adobe is on about 80% of devices worldwide - heh heh my opinions coming out :-) ).

We're serving photos from Google (an application called "Picasa"). This means that the photos are actually stored out there on the web ("in the cloud") somewhere, which is really unimportant to us.

What we're really interested in is how we get our photos off our local computer and uploaded so that people viewing blue mountain can see them.

We use Picasa by having an account at Google called "bmaphotostream@gmail.com" with a password of "!Gibsonge05". Then we download the Picasa Application from the Picasa Website.

We use the Picasa application on our computer to locate our photos, edit them, then upload them to Google. The rest is done behind the scenes, and they will automatically appear at the bluemountain website.
