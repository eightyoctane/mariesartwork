Why register at Blue Mountain Airpark?

Although Blue Mountain Airpark Events can be viewed by anyone, user registration gives you access to our Projects pages which contain photos, movies, and commentaries on projects that range from helicopters to experimental aircraft, and restorations, including fabric projects. If you wish, you can choose to receive emails from us regarding upcoming events and activities.

Registration is prerequisite to landing at Blue Mountain Airpark.
Who is involved at Blue Mountain Airpark?

Basically we have three different categories of community members and associates and all kinds of people. We have local residents and neighbors, airpark property owners and residents, commercial pilots, private pilots, flight instructors, test pilots, engineers, business owners and aircraft mechanics.

Blue Mountain Airpark visitors experience Blue Mountain flying activities, events, and the various kinds of aircraft that frequent the field. Some events include activities like pancake breakfasts, or brunches in the community area. We offer airplane rides for our local neighbors and young people with an interest in aircraft, flying, and careers in the world of aviation. Depending on your level of interest, you may just want to visit our site periodically, or you may wish to register to experience our projects area and receive emails regarding upcoming events.

Only registered Blue Mountain Airpark Property Owner/Residents are permitted access to the "Residents" section.
