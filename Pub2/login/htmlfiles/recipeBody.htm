Blue Mountain Recipe Database, Meal Planner, and Restaurant Guide This is:
  • A database of recipes we create right here at the airpark
  • A weekly and monthly meal planner
  • A restaurant list and review guide created by the residents of Blue Mountain Airpark

Once you get into it, I think you will find this application quite sophisticated and capable. Of course the price for this functionality is some level of complication.

More detail:

Recipes
Researching this and thinking about it a bit, a recipe can range anywhere from "let's throw it together from memory", to abstract chemistry. So how can we contribute to this database without making it overwhelmingly complicated? Start simple. If you have a fantastic recipe or idea you want to share with other Blue Mountain residents, get it on here as simply and quickly as possible. You don't have to fill in all the fields - that can happen later - or never, meaning that we all talk frequently, and someone can give you a call if they need more detail. The main thing here I think, is we all need more recipe ideas, so let's help each other out.

Restaurants
Marie and I were talking in the car one day about La Fayette restaurants and we decided that if we didn't spread it around a bit (meaning only eating at the same restaurant) - it might soon be the only restaurant left in town. The more we share about good La Fayette restaurants - the longer they'll be around.
