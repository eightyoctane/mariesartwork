Preliminary Vision Statement
Dan Budde (2011):

“Blue Mountain Air Park is a rural aviation-oriented community where owners and guests can pursue the joy of flying and aviation related activities in a fun and safe environment.  Our core values show pride of ownership without being flashy or extravagant because we believe that protects and enhances our property values and promotes a highly cohesive community.”

Item Method
Mower Repair/Service BMA owns two Zero Turn Mowers, a 52", and a 72". Service includes, gas and oil, lubrication, and sharpening the blades. The association also owns additional equipment including a blower and weed whacker. Currently managed and accomplished by the Daters. Financial burden is tracked via yearly budget/expense reports and recovered via monthly Association dues 
Mowing/Trimming Currently managed by the Daters. Daters are final authority. 1) mower checkout procedure in place, 2) mower signup sheet in place, 3) volunteer/adhoc mowing procedure is in place, 4) hours tracked via timesheet and mower hour meter,
Pest Control Currently gophers and fire ants - 1) financial burden acknowledged and recovered via yearly budget and expense, 2) work accomplished adhoc/volunteer
Community, Trail and Pond Community areas including road used by residents, community picnic area, community pond area, road to the pond area, pond parking area, and trail around the airpark perimeter. Trees across the trail,  trees down in the community area. Not yet recognized by the Board as a formal responsibility, adhoc/volunteer
Runway Runway seeding and fertilization, weed removal - 1) One-time seeding accomplished in 2009, 2) no formal on-going plan
Road Adhoc/volunteer, refers to edging, weed whacking, and grass coming up through the asphalt
 
Airpark Entrance No Standard Power, lighting, gate and fence painting, electric gate maintenance, entrance flower box - 1) adhoc/volunteer only, 2) not formally recognized  
Runway and windsock Maintenance No Standard Runway lighting and metal markers, windsock material, and bearings (lubrication and replacement), windsock poll painting - Adhoc/volunteer  
Community and pond Maintenance No Standard Pond benches, wood deck structures, community and pond area chairs, not formally recognized/adhoc  
Fence Maintenance No Standard Not formally recognized  
Road Repair No Standard Not formally recognized, no ongoing plan
