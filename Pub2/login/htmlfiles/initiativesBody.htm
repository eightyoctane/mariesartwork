Proposals (Full View)

 

 At our BMA website, we can communicate new ideas and gain support for initiatives we believe will benefit the future of our community.

Generally, it works like this. Initiatives can be proposed here. Other members can make comments including support, opposition and alternative ideas. Evolved initiatives with enough support will attract leadership. A supported initiative evolves into a committee with a committee leader and members which take action and create milestones toward bringing the initiative to fruition.

For example, the first and most basic initiative which became a reality at Blue Mountain was the list of  Restrictive Covenants, which establish a baseline standard for owner architectural development and property maintenance within the airpark. The Covenants are only a minimal standard and support the spirit, communication, and collaborative approach for new endeavors within our community.  At this time, the Covenants are largely managed by the Architectural Committee and regulated by the Board of Directors.

Committee Chairmen can create new projects with team members. People on a project have access to the project folder and can add comments and upload files related to the project.
