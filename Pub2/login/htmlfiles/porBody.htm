Property Owner and Resident Section

 Version 2.4 12/06/2011

Welcome to the BMA Property Owner section of our website. Here we have subject areas that are interesting to our Property Owners and our Airpark Residents.

Documents/FAQ
Basically any information needed by our residents. Everyone is welcome to contribute or provide input. Everyone has access to this area.
 
Airpark Care Section
The Airpark Care Committee maintains and prioritizes the "Airpark Maintenance Task List", and lobbies for a fraction of the monthly airpark dues for maintenance and improvements. Information concerning lawn mowing and care activities, including task lists, proposals, etc. Everyone has access to this area.
 
Committees
Active Committee Section. If you are on a committee like the Architectural Landscaping and Control Committee, you have access to one or more areas in this section. The Committee Chairman controls information in this section.
 
Chairmen
Accessible to Chairmen. Chairmen can add and delete Committee members, etc.
 
Board Members
Accessible to Booard Members. Contains documentation and other information relevant for the management of the Community and surrounding neighbors. Contains historical information and guidance information from past board members which will be useful for future Board Members. Website tools which are used to create new Committees for the Committees section, and tools required to populate the Chairmen Section.
 
Website Admin
Tools accessible to the website admin for management of the BMA website.
